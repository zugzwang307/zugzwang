WHIPCASH

PROJECT NAME

AUGUST 2021 – OCTOBER 2022

REALIZATION

ABOUT

An online marketplace in which purchase and sale is carried out through transactions. The roles of buyer, seller and manager were implemented within the framework of the service.

Given the mentality and specifics of US customers, a survey was conducted of similar marketplaces. This helped define interface and interaction patterns.

USER FLOW

The transaction process was designed to ensure security and trust between buyers and sellers, with clear steps and feedback at each stage.

RESULTS

The final design provided a clear, trustworthy experience for all user roles, reducing friction in transactions and increasing platform adoption.
